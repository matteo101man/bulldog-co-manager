## 2. MISSION

UGA Bulldog Battalion conducts comprehensive leadership development, physical fitness training, and military science instruction in order to develop future Army officers and maintain unit readiness.

---

## 3. EXECUTION

### a. Commander's Intent

**Purpose:** Develop cadets into competent, confident leaders capable of serving as commissioned officers in the United States Army.

**Key Tasks:**
- Execute weekly physical training (PT) program
- Conduct leadership laboratories (Labs)
- Maintain accountability and discipline
- Develop tactical and leadership competencies
- Prepare cadets for Advanced Camp and commissioning

**End State:** Cadets are physically fit, tactically proficient, and demonstrate leadership competencies required for commissioning as Army officers.

### b. Concept of Operations

**Phase 1: Weekly Training Cycle**
- Monday-Friday: Physical Training (PT) as scheduled
- Thursday: Leadership Laboratory
- Tuesday: Tactics Training (MS3s)
- Continuous: Academic instruction and professional development

**Phase 2: Special Training Events**
- Field Training Exercises (FTX)
- Leadership Development Exercises (LDX)
- Ranger Challenge training
- Community service events

**Phase 3: Assessment and Evaluation**
- Physical fitness assessments
- Leadership evaluations
- Academic performance tracking
- Attendance accountability

### c. Tasks to Subordinate Units

**All Companies:**
- Execute PT schedule as published
- Maintain 100% accountability
- Report attendance through chain of command
- Coordinate with S-4 for logistics support
- Complete supply tracker NLT 2 weeks prior to scheduled labs
- Enforce uniform and appearance standards

**Alpha Company:**
- Execute assigned training events
- Maintain company discipline and readiness
- Coordinate internally for pre/post lab logistics

**Bravo Company:**
- Execute assigned training events
- Maintain company discipline and readiness
- Coordinate internally for pre/post lab logistics

**Charlie Company:**
- Execute assigned training events
- Maintain company discipline and readiness
- Coordinate internally for pre/post lab logistics

**Ranger Company:**
- Execute assigned training events
- Maintain company discipline and readiness
- Coordinate internally for pre/post lab logistics
- Conduct additional PT sessions (Monday/Friday)

**Grizzly Company:**
- Execute assigned training events
- Maintain company discipline and readiness
- Coordinate internally for pre/post lab logistics
- Coordinate with BN HQ for training event participation

**Headquarters Company:**
- Provide staff support to all companies
- Coordinate battalion-level training events
- Maintain battalion accountability
- Execute S-1 through S-6 functions

### d. Coordinating Instructions

**Uniform Standards:**
- PT: Summer PTs or Winter PTs as directed
- Lab: OCPs with water source
- Special events: As specified in event CONOP

**Accountability:**
- Report all absences through chain of command
- Excused absences require prior approval
- Unexcused absences subject to disciplinary action

**Safety:**
- Enforce heat mitigation measures during hot weather
- Maintain hydration standards
- Follow risk management procedures
- Report all injuries immediately

**Communication:**
- Primary: Chain of command
- Emergency: Contact BN HQ immediately
- Updates: Via official battalion communication channels

**Timeline:**
- Weekly training cycle: Monday through Sunday
- Special events: As published in training schedule
- Deadlines: As specified in individual taskings
